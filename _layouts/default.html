Who am I?

I am a sophemore at Washington University in St. Louis studying Computer Science. I only started coding a year ago but my interest in it has motivated me to learn very much since then. My passion for computers came from when I was young and whenever anything was wrong with the family computer, I was always eager to be the repair-man. I enjoy learning new information on my own and have built several projects from scratch. I hope to pursue software development in the future as both a career and a hobby. I am actively looking to get my first Software Engineering job/internship this summer so that I can gain experience that will allow me to improve and learn as much as I can.

Life

I grew up in the suburbs of Pittsburgh and have been a huge Steeler/Penguins/Pirates fan for as long as I can remember. I went to Pittsburgh Central Catholic High School where I played Baseball, Basketball and Soccer. I graduated in 2015 and was recruited to come to Wash U to play on the soccer team. I decided in the summer after my freshman year at Wash U that I would study Computer Science because I loved the feeling of solving a tough coding problem and was anxious to be on the cutting edge of technology.
